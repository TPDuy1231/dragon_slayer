ABOUT

Dragon Slayer-GameFi

DRAGON SLAYER's content revolves around ancient European warriors searching for and destroying dragons that are currently roaming everywhere. The goal is because the dragon is a sacred animal with extraordinary power that no mortal can touch. Therefore, players must go through harsh training periods to accumulate enough knowledge, experience and experience to be able to defeat them. One side is to slay the dragon because it stores a huge amount of treasure, and on the other hand, it is necessary to defend the house to avoid invasion in the chaotic era.
